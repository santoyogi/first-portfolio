Pokemon: A Brief Guide

Pokemon is a role play video game published by Nintendo and developed by Game Freak. This RPG follows a character who explores their region while collecting and battling the Pokemon they catch. This franchise is huge and has multiple tv shows, movies, books and games. As popular as it is; there are some who have never played this game. For those who are new, I will review two key topics to get them started.

Starter Pokemon

All Pokemon franchise games share a common start. The player is given the option to pick their very first Pokemon of the game. These are called "Starter Pokemon", there are always 3 of them. Each generation, the three Pokemon vary, but are almost always the same type:

  • fire
  • water
  • grass

Whichever Pokemon chosen, decides what Pokemon their rival player will choose. If the player chooses fire then their rival will pick water and so forth. You can read more about Pokemon Starters on the Bulbagarden site.

Pokemon types

As previously mentioned, Pokemon have "types". These types refer to elemental properties associated with the Pokemon and their moves. There is a total of 18 total types.
